Regular Expressions!

Towards the end of the semester, we learned about regular expressions, commonly refered to as regex. We can use regex to find patterns in strings using algorithms and functions such as re.findall. Regex can be applied in a biological context in many way. Below I will demontrate just one of these ways.

Restriction Enzyme Example

Here is an example from one of our homeworks. Here, I used re.findall to match the restriction enzyme binding sites ANTAAT and GCRWTG in a chunk of nucleotides.
